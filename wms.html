Expiry & Lot Monitoring Open
The Inventory → Expiry & Lots page tracks all lot-controlled stock. Colour-coded alerts fire automatically based on days-to-expiry thresholds. For F&B items the system enforces FEFO at pick time regardless of operator preference.
